Founders

Laura and her friends has always been fond of spas. However, they noticed how expensive it was for a trip down to the spa. She realized that she can easily have a DIY spa day in the comfort of her own home. She purchases many different things to add to her bath from different businesses. In order to make those items more convenient to purchase, she came up with Luxury Soak

 




Our Goal

Many of us have tiring days and very limited time to rest. Our goal is to make shopping for baths much easier and affordable. Simply use our easy to navigate website and add-to-cart anything that catches your eye! Each product line provides a quick explanation of their benefits to your skin.
